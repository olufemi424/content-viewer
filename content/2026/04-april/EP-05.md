### [8–44 sec] THE 7-DAY PLAN

> **"Day 1: Ask AI one question you'd normally Google. That's it. Just try it.**
>
> Day 2: Ask it to rewrite one email or message you wrote today — and use its version.**
>
> Day 3: Give it a task you've been putting off. Ask it to break it into steps.**
>
> Day 4: Summarize something long. A document, a video transcript, an article. Done in seconds.**
>
> Day 5: Ask it to explain something you've never fully understood — like how taxes work, or what inflation actually means.**
>
> Day 6: Use it to plan something. A weekend, a meal prep, a budget. Let it think with you.**
>
> Day 7: Now combine two of those. Pick any two things from this week — and do them back to back."**

*[VISUAL: Day counter on screen (Day 1, Day 2...) as you say each one. Keep them short on screen.]*
