### [3–15 sec] THE SPECTRUM

> **"There's actually a massive hierarchy happening right now.**
>
> **At the very top?** People using AI every single hour — building businesses, writing code, creating content at lightning speed.
>
> **In the middle?** People using it to plan their week, answer emails, or get recipe ideas.
>
> **Lower down?** People who've only heard the word 'ChatGPT' once or twice.
>
> **And right at the bottom?** Entire communities that haven't even heard of AI yet — because internet or electricity is still a luxury."

*[VISUAL: Ladder graphic rises on screen — icons appear at each level as you speak]*
- Top rung icon: laptop / code
- Middle rung icon: phone / calendar
- Lower rung icon: person scrolling, confused
- Bottom rung icon: village / no device

---

### [15–28 sec] THE ANALOGY

> **"It's exactly like how smartphones spread.**
>
> Some people can't live without them.
>
> While others in remote villages are still using basic feature phones — or none at all.
>
> AI is doing the exact same thing right now. Just faster."

*[VISUAL: Split screen — someone using AI tools on a MacBook vs. a simple feature phone]*

---

### [28–40 sec] WHY IT MATTERS

> **"The crazy part? The gap between the top and the bottom is growing every single day.**
>
> **But here's the good news:**
>
> No matter where you are on that ladder… you can climb it."

*[VISUAL: Ladder graphic again — a small figure climbing upward]*

### [40–50 sec] CTA + TEASER
