# Practice Speaking (Record Every Clip)

## Communication Structure
- What? Your idea, your belief, your artwork.
- So what? Why it matters to the person you’re talking to.
- Now what? What comes next—show, invite, or ask for action.

## Daily Practice
- Master reading one piece every day.
